Aaj tumne kaha, “baat karna accha nahi lag raha…” Aur sach kahun, us pal thoda sa chup ho gaya main. Yaad aaya woh waqt jab choti choti baaton par tumhari hasi phone ke is side tak aa jaati thi. Kabhi ek simple “hmm” bhi itna khaas lagta tha, jaise din ki thakan wahi khatam ho jaati ho. Kabhi sochta hoon… kya woh pal sirf meri yaadon mein reh gaye hain? Ya tumhe bhi kabhi achanak woh baatein yaad aati hain— late night messages, bina wajah ki hasi, aur woh chhoti si baat jo hum dono ko khush kar deti thi. Main apne dil ki baatein tumse isliye share karta tha, kyunki mujhe lagta tha tum samjhogi, judge nahi karogi. Par shayad meri baat kehne ka tareeqa hi galat tha… agar usse tumhe takleef hui ho, toh dil se sorry. Main shikayat nahi kar raha… bas samajhna chahta hoon. Kya meri baatein sach mein itni badal gayi hain, ya tumhare dil mein unke liye jagah thodi si kam ho gayi hai? Kyuki sach bataun, kuch log aadat ban jaate hain zindagi ki… aur jab woh khamosh ho jaate hain, toh sirf baatein hi nahi, bahut si achchi yaadein bhi chup si ho jaati hain.
“Some words are easier to write than to say.”
